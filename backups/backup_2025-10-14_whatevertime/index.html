📋 Building Rules & Requirements

🏠 Residential Zones

  • Cost: 60 wood + 8 ore + 15 goods
  • Requirements: Must be next to operable roads
  • Restrictions: Cannot be within 3 tiles of industrial zones
  • Consumption: Consumes 1 good per second

🏢 Commercial Zones

  • Cost: 30 wood + 10 ore
  • Requirements: Must be next to operable roads AND within 5x5 area of power
  • Production: Produces 0.5 goods per second (needs materials + power)

🏭 Industrial Zones

  • Cost: 40 wood + 20 ore
  • Requirements: Must have water access within 1 tile AND be adjacent to power
  • Production: Produces 1 material per second (needs wood + ore + power)

🏬 Mixed Use Zones

  • Cost: 44 wood + 24 ore
  • Requirements: Must be within 5x5 area of power
  • Production: Produces 0.5 materials + 0.5 goods per second

🛣️ Roads & Bridges

  • Roads: Cost 4 wood, must connect to industrial zones or other roads
  • Bridges: Cost 16 wood + 6 ore, can only be placed on water next to roads
  • Action Cost: Roads and bridges cost 0.5 actions each

⚡ Power Infrastructure

  • Power Plants: Cost 25 wood + 15 ore, need water access or adjacent power plants
  • Power Lines: Cost 3 wood + 1 ore, must be within 5 tiles of power plants
  • Coverage: Power plants provide 5x5 area coverage

🏗️ Resource Buildings

  • Lumber Yards: Cost 10 wood, must be within 3 tiles of forests
  • Mining Outposts: Cost 20 wood + 10 ore, must be within 1 tile of mountains
  • Production: Both produce 0.5 resources per second

🚫 General Rules

  • Natural Terrain: Cannot build on water, forests, or mountains (except bridges on water)
  • Replacement: Cannot replace existing buildings - must erase first
  • Erase Tool: Free to use, available even with 0 actions
  • Bridges: When erased, return to water instead of grassland

🎓 Building Progression Tutorial

🏗️ Step 1: Resource Production

Start by building these two essential resource producers:

  • 🌲 Lumber Yard: Cost 10 wood, must be within 3 tiles of forests
  • ⛏️ Mining Outpost: Cost 20 wood + 10 ore, must be within 1 tile of mountains

These provide the basic resources (wood & ore) you'll need for everything else!

⚡ Step 2: Power Infrastructure

Build power infrastructure to supply energy to your city:

  • ⚡ Power Plant: Cost 25 wood + 15 ore, needs water access
  • 🔌 Power Lines: Cost 3 wood + 1 ore, extend power coverage

Power is essential - without it, your industrial zones won't work!

🏭 Step 3: Industrial Zones

Build industrial zones to process raw materials:

  • 🏭 Industrial Zone: Cost 40 wood + 20 ore, needs power + water access
  • 🛣️ Roads: Cost 0.5 actions each, connect industrial zones

Industrial zones convert wood + ore into processed materials!

🏪 Step 4: Commercial Zones

Build commercial zones to produce goods:

  • 🏪 Commercial Zone: Cost 30 wood + 15 ore + 10 processed materials
  • Requirements: Must be connected to operable roads + within power coverage

Commercial zones convert processed materials into goods!

🏠 Step 5: Residential Zones

Finally, build residential zones for your population:

  • 🏠 Residential Zone: Cost 60 wood + 8 ore + 15 goods
  • Requirements: Must be connected to operable roads + away from industrial zones

Residential zones consume goods but provide population for your city!

💡 Pro Tips

  • Power Management: 1 power plant can supply 2 industrial zones
  • Road Networks: Roads become inoperable if industrial zones lose power
  • Resource Balance: Monitor your resource production vs consumption
  • Strategic Placement: Plan your city layout for efficient resource flow
